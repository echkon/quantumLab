Quantum Lab

Our lab focuses on advancing the frontiers of quantum science and technology through four key research areas:

  • Quantum Algorithms: Developing novel algorithms tailored for quantum computers to solve complex problems more efficiently.
  • Foundational Physics: Investigating fundamental principles of quantum mechanics to deepen our understanding of the quantum world.
  • Quantum Metrology and Sensing: Enhancing measurement precision by leveraging unique quantum properties, pushing beyond classical limits.
  • Quantum Measurement: Designing precise measurement techniques at the quantum scale, including exploring nonlocal measurement methods.

By integrating these areas, our goal is to innovate practical quantum technologies that improve sensing, computation, and fundamental understanding of nature.
