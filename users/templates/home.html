Welcome to Quantum Lab @ Tohoku University

We conduct cutting-edge research in quantum sensing, quantum metrology, and quantum information science. Our lab aims to push the limits of precision measurements by leveraging quantum technologies such as spin squeezing, multiparameter estimation, and entanglement-enhanced detection schemes.

Located at Tohoku University, we collaborate with institutions around the world and welcome students and researchers who are passionate about the quantum future.
